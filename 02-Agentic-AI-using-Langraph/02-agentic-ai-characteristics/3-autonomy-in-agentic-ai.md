Autonomy is the defining characteristic of **Agentic AI**.

This document explains autonomy in Agentic AI across three key facets:

---

## ‚öôÔ∏è 1. Autonomy in Execution

**Execution autonomy** means the Agentic AI can carry out tasks **end-to-end without continuous human intervention**.

### How It Works

- The AI receives a **goal**, not step-by-step instructions
- It breaks the goal into a plan
- It executes each step sequentially or in parallel
- It tracks progress and completion status

### Example (Recruitment Scenario)

- Goal: _Hire a backend engineer_
- AI executes:
  1. Create a job description
  2. Post the job on LinkedIn
  3. Monitor applications
  4. Shortlist candidates
  5. Schedule interviews

All actions are executed **without the user manually triggering each step**.

---

## üß† 2. Autonomy in Decision Making

**Decision-making autonomy** allows the Agentic AI to **choose actions based on observed outcomes and reasoning**, rather than fixed rules.

### How It Works

- The AI evaluates real-world signals
- Compares progress against expectations
- Selects the next best action
- Revises its strategy dynamically

### Example

After posting a job:

- The AI observes:
  - Low application count
  - Poor candidate quality
- The AI decides to:
  - Rewrite the job description
  - Adjust required skills
  - Change the job title

These decisions are made **internally by the AI**, not prompted by a user.

---

## üõ†Ô∏è 3. Autonomy in Tool Usage

**Tool autonomy** refers to the AI‚Äôs ability to **select, invoke, and chain tools on its own** to accomplish tasks.

### How It Works

- The AI understands available tools
- Chooses the appropriate tool for each step
- Uses tools in sequence or combination
- Interprets tool outputs and acts on them

### Example

In the recruitment workflow, the Agentic AI may autonomously:

- Use LinkedIn API to post a job
- Query an Applicant Tracking System (ATS)
- Use email or calendar tools to schedule interviews
- Use analytics tools to evaluate candidate performance

The AI decides:

- **Which tool to use**
- **When to use it**
- **How to use the output**
